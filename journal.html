Issue Zero

How this page will work

The goal is not to ship a crowded dashboard. It is to collect chess data work in a more editorial format: slower, clearer, and easier to revisit.

Each entry can eventually combine a chart, a short written claim, the sample definition, and a few notes about what the data does and does not say.

Good chess data pages should feel less like control panels and more like field notes.

For now, this page is a prepared shell. Later it can hold player dossiers, opening notebooks, tournament recaps, or any chart-led story you want to publish.

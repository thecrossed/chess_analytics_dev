Blindfold Chess Voice MVP includes Stockfish as a third-party chess engine.

Stockfish is licensed under the GNU General Public License version 3 (GPLv3).

This app uses an unmodified Stockfish binary as an external UCI engine process.
The Stockfish binary is not linked into the app and is not modified by this app.

Users may replace the Stockfish binary in resources/engines/ with another
compatible Stockfish binary.

Stockfish source code is available from the official Stockfish project.

Stockfish version: Stockfish 18
Binary target: macOS arm64 / Darwin arm64 local development binary

How to Read Eval and Best Move in PGN Analysis

Published: 2026-03-13

What eval score means

In this tool, eval score is the engine's evaluation after the played move. Positive values generally favor White, negative values favor Black.

What best move means

Best move and best move eval are calculated from the position before the played move. This lets you compare what was played versus what the engine preferred in the same position.

How to use both together

Focus on moves where played eval and best-move eval differ a lot. Those are usually the most valuable training moments.
